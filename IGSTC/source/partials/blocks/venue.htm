The conference will be held at the Centre for Industrial Consultancy and Sponsored Research (ICSR), Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai. IIT Madras is one among the foremost institutes of national importance in higher technological education, basic and applied research. It has been the top-ranking engineering institute in India for the past 7 years in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), Government of India since this ranking system was announced in 2015. Much of the campus is a protected forest, carved out of the Guindy National Park, home to many animals that are not commonly seen in a busy city like Chennai.

Location: The campus is conveniently located about 10 km from Chennai airport and 12 km from Chennai central railway station and is well connected by city buses, sub-urban, and metro train services. Kasturba Nagar is the nearest station on the Chennai MRTS line and Little mount is the nearest station in the Chennai Metro line. Auto and taxi services are also easily accessible to the campus.
